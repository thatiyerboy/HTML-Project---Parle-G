ABOUT US

Since 1929, we have grown to become India's leading manufacturer of biscuits and confectionery.

As the makers of the world's largest selling biscuit, Parle-G, and a host of other very popular brands, the Parle name symbolizes quality, nutrition and superior taste.

An in-depth understanding of the Indian consumer psyche has helped us develop a marketing philosophy that reflects the needs of the Indian masses. We have made it a tradition to deliver both health and taste, with a value-for-money positioning that allows people from all classes and age groups to enjoy Parle products to the fullest. With a reach spanning the remotest villages of India and major cities across the world, the House of Parle has become synonymous with trust, globally.
